Blue Structure Pay Stations and Guidelines

Payment methods, parking rules, and important information for visitors.

Northwest Corner of Level A

This pay-on-foot station accepts both cash and credit card payments. The pay station is located in the northwest corner of Level A. Depending on operational needs, payments may also be collected by an attendant at the kiosk upon entry to the Blue Structure.

Get Directions

Northeast Corner of Level A & Exit Terminals

This pay station accepts credit card payments only and is located in the northeast corner of Level A. A lost ticket fee applies. Payments may also be collected at the entry kiosk based on operational needs.

Get Directions

Scan QR Code

Scan the QR code on your ticket and follow the prompts to pay by credit card.

Exit the Parking Area

Pay with cash

EV Charging & Guideline

Please see below for EV charging details and terms of use

B

Blue Structure

EV charging stations are located in the Blue Structure at 3855 S Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90037, and are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Charger availability is as follows:

  • Level A: 2 Dual Chargers
  • Level B: 2 Single Chargers and 11 Dual Chargers

To ensure access for all guests, please limit charging to a maximum of three (3) hours and move your vehicle promptly once charging is complete.

Event Parking Information

Parking rates, availability, and rules may change during special events.

Special Event Rates

  • Event rates vary by type and are on the organizer’s website.

  • Minimum rate for special event parking after 5pm is $24.

  • The parking fee is a flat rate, regardless of the duration of stay.

  • All parking payments are non-refundable.

  • NO in and out allowed.

Click here for special event rates

Oversized Vehicle Parking

  • Oversized parking is subject to availability.

  • Oversized parking will be charged at the event rate per space occupied.

  • Guests should pre-purchase oversized parking from the organizer.

  • Vehicles requiring multiple spaces must purchase multiple parking passes.
