Pay Stations and Guidelines

Payment methods, parking rules, and important information for visitors.

Northeast Corner of Level A & Exit Terminals

Credit card payments are accepted at the pay station located in the northeast corner of Level A. Payments are upfront at kiosks when the system is down. All parking payments are non-refundable. Lost ticket fee applies.

Northwest Corner of Level A

Both Cash and credit card payments are accepted at the pay station located in the northwest corner of Level A. Payments are upfront at kiosks when the system is down. All parking payments are non-refundable. Lost ticket fee applies.

Event Parking Information

Parking rates, availability, and rules may change during special events.

Special Event Rates

  • Event rates vary by type and are on the organizer’s website.

  • Minimum rate for special event parking after 5pm is $24.

  • The parking fee is a flat rate, regardless of the duration of stay.

  • All parking payments are non-refundable.

  • NO in and out allowed.

Click here for special event rates

Oversized Vehicle Parking

  • Oversized parking is subject to availability.

  • Oversized parking will be charged at the event rate per space occupied.

  • Guests should pre-purchase oversized parking from the organizer.

  • Vehicles requiring multiple spaces must purchase multiple parking passes.
